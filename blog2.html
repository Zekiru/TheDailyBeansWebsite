Small and Simple Acts

A Blog Post by Ponce Yoro, May 23 2021.

SDGs, or also known as The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all".

The SDGs were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by the year 2030. Each goal having their own section and purpose, depending on what part of the world you are in the SDGs needed for you may differ.

So, how can the youth contribute to achieve these goals? Do the specific goals that you feel like your country needs the most? Although these kinds of things are easier said than done. Start small and simple, try donating to charities and give to the needy. Participate in conservation and protect Mother Earth. Protect the endangered animal species from extinction.

Small contributions may not seem like much at first, but they can really help the world long-term. The youth is the future of our society, they renew, refresh and rebuild our world, introducing new innovations, leadership roles, life skills etc.

“The power of youth is the commonwealth for the entire world. The faces of young people are the faces of our past, our present and our future. No segment in the society can match with the power, idealism, enthusiasm, and courage of the young people.

– Kailash Satyarthi
