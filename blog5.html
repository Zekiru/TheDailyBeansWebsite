Helping The Youth to Contribute

A Blog Post by Zyan Kenviec Catabay, May 22 2021.

Our lives are only temporary, achieving worldwide long-term goals will really be difficult for us and for the world to achieve. The United nations has released the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) which will be achieved by the people living in our worlds. These mentioned goals are to be achieved not just to simply manifest the unity between the nations but also to strengthen the pillar for the future generations.

We all know that in achieving these goals, it will be spearheaded by different leaders in different countries. It is really evident that goals such as these is big and will make a huge impact in the whole world.

We all dream for a utopian-type of world, but we are far from achieving such state. The United Nations are aiming for a brighter future with the help of launching the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. In achieving such a world, we must know that not only the leaders of different countries should be the one doing all the work, but we must also contribute to achieve a huge impact for the future.

The youth should not be neglected in these kinds of situations, the creativity, passion, and dedication of the youth will surprisingly make a huge impact in the world that we are all dreaming for. The question remains on how the youth will contribute to achieve the goals mentioned, it is a common knowledge for all of us that the creativity they possess is incomparable to most of the people.

The youth are the ones who will lead the future generations to come, and that is why it is the duty of those who are in power to help these children to contribute a huge impact for the society and the world.

Being able to know and experience such things during your younger years, will really help you in understanding the realities of the world and will help you in visualizing long-term goals and solutions for the problems that we are experiencing. Let us all make a huge impact for the betterment of the society and the world.
